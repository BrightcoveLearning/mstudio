Uploading and Publishing Your First Video

{{ page.description }}

Brightcove Marketing Studio makes it easy to upload videos and then generate the embed code needed to publish the video on an HTML page.

Uploading videos

Brightcove Marketing Studio makes it easy to upload your videos. To upload videos to your account, follow these steps.

  1. Click Media in the primary navigation.
  2. Click Upload. The uploader panel will open in the page.
  3. Click Browse for Videos and browse and select a video to upload.
  4. After files are selected, the upload progress will display. Click Edit to view the video properties.

When the upload is complete, an Uploaded status will display. This indicates the video upload has completed and it is safe to navigate away from the Videos page.

For complete details on uploading videos, see Uploading Videos.

Editing video details

To view and edit video details, follow these steps:

  1. Click on a video title to open the video details page.
For complete details on editing video details, see Editing Video Details.

Publishing a video

Publishing a video will generate the HTML embed code needed to display your video on a web page. To publish a video, follow these steps:

  1. In card view, hover over a card, click the action button (...) and then click Embed on website.
  2. The Embed on website dialog will open. Optionally, click Preview to open a preview of the video in another browser tab.
  3. Click the Copy link to copy the HTML code. This is the code that needs to be pasted into your webpage to display the video.
  4. Click Done.
