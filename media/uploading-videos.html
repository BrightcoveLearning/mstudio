Uploading Videos

{{ page.description }}

To upload videos to your account, follow these steps.

  1. In the primary navigation, click Media.
  2. Drag files from the file system to the Media page. The body of the page becomes the drop zone.
  3. Click the status drawer icon in the page header to view the upload progress.
Note: There is no file size limit. Brightcove has tested with uploads as large as 100GB without any issues.

To cancel the uploads, click Cancel uploads. While uploads are in an uploading state, do not close or move away from the Media page. Doing so may cause an incomplete upload.

Other tasks can be performed on the Media page while uploads are in progress. When all uploads are complete, the status drawer will display a green circle . This indicates the video uploads have completed and it is safe to navigate away from the Media page.
