Uploading Videos

{{ page.description }}

To upload videos to your account, follow these steps.

  1. Click Media in the primary navigation.
  2. Drag files from the file system to the Media page. The whole becomes the drop zone.
  3. Click the upload status indicator in the page header to view the upload progress.
Note: There is no file size limit. Brightcove has tested with uploads as large as 100GB without any issues.

To cancel the upload, click Cancel Upload. Click Dismiss to remove the file entry from the page. While uploads are in an uploading state, do not close or move away from the Media page. Doing so may cause an incomplete upload.

When the upload is complete, an Uploaded status will display. This indicates the video upload has completed and it is safe to navigate away from the Media page.

All uploaded videos will have a status of Active. To learn more about video properties, see Editing Video Details.
