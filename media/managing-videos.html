To access the videos in your account, login to Video Cloud Studio and click Media in the primary navigation.

By default, the videos in the account will be displayed as a series of cards. The view icons at the top of the page can be used to switch between card view and list view.

Using card view

To view the videos in the account as a series of cards, click the card view icon at the top of the page.

Each card will display the following information:

  • Video poster image
  • Video length
  • Video title
  • Created date and time
  • Number of video tags

Using list view

Click the list view icon at the top of the page to view a list of videos in the account.

The body of the page will display the following:

  • Title
  • Status
    • ACTIVE - Indicates the video is active and can be played
    • INACTIVE - Indicates the video is inactive (can't be played)
  • Video ID - System assigned video ID (unique across all Video Cloud accounts)
  • Created date and time
  • Last updated date and time

Editing video properties

To edit the video properties, click on the video name to open the video properties page. See Editing Video Properties for information on editing video properties using the Media module.

Deleting videos

To delete videos, click the checkboxes next to the video thumbnails of the videos to delete and then click the delete button (). Confirm the delete by clicking the Delete button.

If the video has been added to any manual playlists, that will be indicated.

To see which manual playlists the videos is in, click the video title to open the video properties. The Categorization properties will display the manual playlists that the video has been added to.

Publishing videos

Publishing a video will generate embed code that is needed to embed the video in a web page. For more information, see Publishing Videos and Playlists.

Searching for videos

The Search field at the top of the page can be used to search the videos in the account. For more information, see Searching Videos.
