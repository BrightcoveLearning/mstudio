Upload videos

Change Video display
  1. Click an icon at the top right of the page to view the videos as cards or as a list.

Click the button again to close the panel.

View video details
  1. Click on a video poster image or name to open the Video Details page.
Search videos
  1. Enter search terms and press enter.

The following video fields will be searched:

  • Video ID
  • Name
  • Short description
  • Long description
  • Tags
  • Reference ID
  • Custom fields
