Media

{{ page.description }}

Upload videos
  1. Drag and drop a video from the file system to the Media page.
Details
Change page display
  1. Click an icon at the top right of the page to view the videos as cards or as a list.
Search videos
  1. Enter search terms and press enter.

The following video fields will be searched:

  • Video ID
  • Name
Details
Publish videos
  1. Switch to card view.
  2. Hover over the video to publish.
  3. Click the More (...) button and then click Embed on website.
  4. Click the preview link to preview in a new browser tab or copy the embed code.
Details
