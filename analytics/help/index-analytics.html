Analytics

{{ page.description }}

Select a date range
  1. Click the data selector dropdown at the top of the page to select a date range.
Change data display
  1. Click the dropdown above the graph to select a data view.
    • Video Views - The number of times a video started playing
    • Viewed Minutes - Number of minutes the video was watched
    • Play Rate - The percentage of videos that were loaded that were actually played (video views / video impressions)
    • Avg. Completion Rate - The average percent watched per video view (calculated by adding all the percentage watched divided by the number of video views)
  2. Video details are displayed below the graph.
